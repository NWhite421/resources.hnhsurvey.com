House Information
House Model

The correct house model and garage orientation are used on the lot. You can get a typical house model from the Homebuilder Houses.dwg CAD file or by drawing in the Foundation Plan dimensions from plans provided.

House Location

Place the house in between the side lot lines. Place the house model behind the setback line plus 1.00 foot. Ensure the house and appurtenances.

Building Ties

Tie the closest point on each side of the building perpendicularly to the property line. If the perpendicular point falls outside the property, you can tie to the closest property corner.

Finish Floor Elevation

General Requirement Determine the Finished Floor Elevation (FFE) of the house by adding 2.00' above the crown of road directly in front of the subject parcel. Consider your lot's drainage onto both neighboring lots and rights-of-way.

Palm Bay Requirement FFE grades are determined by the septic engineer. The FFE callout shall be removed.

Palm Coast Requirement Follow the grading plan provided in the following PDF.

Ocala Requirement

Generally: The FFE will be provided by the client after the septic permit is provided.

For Commence Logistics: The client has opted to bear the responsibility of determining the FFE. The FFE shall be removed from the survey.

Driveway

Add a driveway from the garage to the road. If the house does not have a garage, do not add one. Ensure the apron of the driveway lies outside of the property.

Grading Information
Spot Elevations

General Requirement Create eight (8) proposed grades on the parcel showing the intended drainage. Have three elevations in the rear of the lot, three elevations in the front of the lot, and two elevations in the middle on each side lot line.

Palm Bay Requirement See the sample for a reference of elevations.

Palm Coast Requirement See the sample for a reference of elevations.

Palm Coast Requirement The client has opted to bear the responsibility of determining the final grades. Do not add any grades.
